Ganesh Chandra Das

Ganesh Chandra Das' compositions mostly focus on landscapes and cityscapes. With his impressionist style, as reflected in his light, loose and broad brushstrokes, he seeks to capture and convey the mood of a scene rather than lend it a photo-realistic quality. The details are fewer, the color palette is minimal, the tones are muted, and the overall visual effect is sketchy but deeply suggestive and evocative. Human presence, too, is reduced to blurred outlines of a few figures; they seem to blend with the surroundings, whether natural or artificial, rather than stand out. They bear a certain degree of spontaneity and minimalism of having been drawn on the spot from live observation, rather than from memory.
