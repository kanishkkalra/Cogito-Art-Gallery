Manoj Kumar Paswan

Art is an apt platform to dissect relevant issues that plague our contemporary world and Paswanâ€™s art is an expression of this. Paswan is a keen observer of the cityscape where individual's become part of the faceless crowd living an unvarying life like clockwork. He looks at the multitude of people who have left homes and families behind to be part of the cityscape which only looks at them with apathy. This indifference finds a representation in his canvas where abstract images are used to deliberate on real issues. He uses muted colours and repetitive images which shed further light on the monotonous human existence. He draws inspiration from the world of ants to represent the ant like survival of the modern man. Together his paintings are a strong source of socio-political commentary on our current scenario.
