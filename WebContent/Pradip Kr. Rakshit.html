Pradip Kumar Rakshit

Pradip Kr Rakshit's composition is a world of vibrancy and life especially in his use of colours. He often uses vivid colours of red and green to bring plants and flowers to life in his paintings. Nature is his source of inspiration as he explores it through his artistic lens. His renditions of these flowers and plants have a realistic note to them at the same time he presents them against a surreal backdrop usually coloured red or black. His paintings use a motif of repetition where a single displacement catches the eye of his viewer and this is the key to his art where the abstract and concrete co-exist.
