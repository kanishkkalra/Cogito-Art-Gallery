Probir Ghosh

The compositions of Probir Ghosh are centred on the ‘human’. He presents man caught in the everydayness of a daily existence. But his choice of colours reflects his larger thematic concerns as well as his source of inspiration. He sets the object of his attention against dull and grey backgrounds but the object itself is painted in vivid colours. This use of contrast evokes a feeling of pleasure even amongst beings caught in the most mundane of tasks. This creates a space for a positive energy and captures the beauty that is present in simplest of things. Hence, here is a celebration of the menial and otherwise tedious episodes of human existence.
