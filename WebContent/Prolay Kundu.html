Prolay Kundu

Prolay Kundu's world constitutes of exploration of a world where nature and manmade structures coexist in harmony. His works present a slice of an unhurried and uncorrupted existence which is still cocooned in innocence. His canvas is a mirror of this artistic vision as he uses realistic imagery to put across his thoughts to his viewers. He finds inspiration in the simple things in life which brings overwhelming happiness like the sea which figure prominently in his paintings. His work is a celebration of these simple pleasures which leave behind lasting marks. There is a sense of joy and peace in his paintings as the integrity of nature remains untouched even when man has left his distinct mark on the landscape.
