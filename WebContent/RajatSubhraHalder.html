Rajat Subhra Halder

Rajat Subra Halder’s world in one which plays on the idea of our perception and reality. By using cleverly positioned lines and objects his pictures give an impression of varied depths. Geometric setting and abstract images become an effective tool in is deft hands to question our version of reality. His use of colours is muted and he uses bright colours only to draw the attention of his viewers to a certain detail. He pictures everyday things like a cat or a vase against an urban background. Though simplistic at first glance, his pictures are a full of satiric overtones. His artistic vision has a memory- like quality to it as the images are born out of reality but are laced in surreal settings.
