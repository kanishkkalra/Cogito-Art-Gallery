Ramendra Nath Kastha

Ramendra Nath Kastha is a printmaker who draws inspiration from many forms of artistic mediums. He uses this to explore the nurturing and protective side of nature whose benevolence he returns by experimenting with non-toxic forms of print making. This celebration of nature extends to his thematic concerns and his use of earthy and muted colours. His works also explores the duality of India experience which races towards modernity but is firmly grounded in ancient philosophy. This imbibes his work with a post-modern outlook which remains rooted in deep spirituality.
