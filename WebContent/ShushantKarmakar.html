Sushanta Karmakar

Sushanta karmakar's compositions are an exploration of the dreamscapes where both fantasies and nightmares find a 'voice' on the canvas. Karmakar uses colours in a deliberate manner to symbolise personal emotions and desires as well as chaos and disorder of the world outside. He uses abstract images that seem haphazard but every single piece fits into the puzzle which manifests into a larger narrative. His lines are smooth giving his painting an element of fluidity. Karmakar frequently challenges his viewers to enter into a journey into their inner psyche where we finds answers as well as ask our own questions.
