Shrikant G. Poddar

The artwork of Srikant G Poddar is an exploration of man's position in the post-modern times. This is a world full of questions not just of the inner mindscape but of the world outside too. In Poddar's composition man and his various roles in relation to individuals and the larger society are dissected to reveal the layers within. Human existence is a combination of many masks and performances. Together these paintings explore this existence through deliberate use of colours which are at times restrained. He lines are neat and precise as is his use of imagery and together he captures individuals caught in a rat race of existence in a world which seems hollow.
