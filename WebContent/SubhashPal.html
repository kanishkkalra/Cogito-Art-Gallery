Subhash Pal

The artworks of Subash Pal are a confluence of austerity with depth of ideas. He uses soft tones of colours against a backdrop of nature to raise questions of identity and fluid borders. They look back at history but are set in contemporary landscapes. They are also a personal journey of introspection which reflects on the larger issues that plague a modern man. His art works are a made up of varied thematic concerns which makes it difficult to box him into a particular label. But this lack of labelling also opens his works to multiple interpretations which becomes a personal experience for the artist as well as the viewer.
