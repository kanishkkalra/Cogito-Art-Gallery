About Us

Cogito Art Gallery aims to provide a platform for established as well as emerging artists. In the current global scenario, there is a pressing need to to facilitate an exchange of ideas. CAG's endeavour is primarily to ensure that indigenous artists receive exposure and access to global markets. Art appeals to our most innate and inherent humanity and we should use it as a unifying force to explore and challenge the limits of our cognitive processes. Every work of art is an act of rebellion, a questioning of the status quo which is essential to our very survival and existence. Artists are willing to take risks while most people exercise caution. It is time to give back to artists: the harbingers of progress who envision the future.
